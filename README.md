# Hi there! I´m René

I'm a sociologist from Chile. My research sits at the intersection of **contentious politics**, **political attitudes**, **social cohesion** and **social stratification** — with a particular focus on how protest and social movements shape attitudes toward institutional change through processes of education and socialization.

Methodologically, I work primarily with quantitative approaches.
